FreeCodeCamp Weather Fetcher

This page will ask for your permission to view current location data. If you do not allow access then get your latitude and longitude from a free service like maps.google.com. Just tap or click a location and the latitude and longitude will appear on-screen. Browsers also allow you to add or remove location access. See help for you specific browser how to do this.

The temperature display is a button. Tap or click it to change from degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit.
